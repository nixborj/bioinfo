# Service Packages

The Bioinformatics team offers several specialized service packages for genomic and phenomic data analysis.

## Available Services

### 1. Variant Calling
Standardized pipelines for identifying variants (SNPs and Indels) from Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS) data.
- Processing of raw sequence data.
- Quality control and mapping.
- Variant detection and annotation.

### 2. High Throughput Phenotyping (HTP) Analysis
Analytical support for data derived from modern phenotyping platforms.
- Image-based phenotyping data processing.
- Extraction of relevant traits and statistical validation.

### 3. Genome-Wide Association Studies (GWAS)
Comprehensive analysis to identify genetic variations associated with specific traits.
- Population structure and kinship analysis.
- Association mapping using various statistical models.
- Identification of significant loci.

### 4. Post GWAS
Downstream analysis following the identification of significant markers.
- Candidate gene identification.
- Functional annotation and pathway analysis.
- Fine mapping support.

### 5. Custom Omics
Tailored bioinformatics solutions for diverse "omics" datasets.
- Transcriptomics (RNA-Seq).
- Specialized data integration and custom pipeline development.
